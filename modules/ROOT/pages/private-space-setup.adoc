== Workflow

. Create the private space. 
. Create the private network.
. Test the private space by deploying an app.
. Configure internal DNS servers, if necessary. 
. Create the VPN. 
. Connect the VPN to your corporate network.
. Customize the private space.


== Before You Begin

* Complete the network admin checklists:

** xref:network-admin-checklist.adoc#private-network-checklist[Private Network Checklist]
** xref:network-admin-checklist.adoc#vpn-checklist[VPN Checklist] 
** xref:network-admin-checklist.adoc#vpn-connection-checklist[VPN Connection Checklist]
** xref:network-admin-checklist.adoc#internal-dns-servers-checklist[Internal DNS Servers Checklist]

* Assign roles and permissions.
